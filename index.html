About us

The Fastest growing fest of Delhi – NCR and Jaipur region, INGENUITY, is the annual cultural fest of NIIT UNIVERSITY. The 3-day event, takes place brings in the spring of the year. Ingenuity, hosts a 2000+ footfall, 750+ participants and 20+ artists. The 72 hours, promise a mix of exhilaration, glitz, drama, and symphony. In the nascent years, we have had star attraction with artists like, Zakir Khan, Nucleya – The band, Sahil Shah, Naalayak – The Band, Nimit Kotian, etc. to gather and engage crowd of all diversity. We host various competitions ranging from Dance, Music, Nukkad and Fashion across various domains of Solo, Duet and Group.
